Joel Embiid

Native of Cameroon…Son of Thomas and Christine Embiid…Has a younger sister, Muriel…Grew up playing mostly volleyball and soccer…According to reports, Luc Richard Mbah a Moute spotted him at a camp in Cameroon in 2011 and influenced his decision to come play high school basketball in the United States… Played his senior year at The Rock School in Gainesville, Florida., averaging 13.0 points, 9.7 rebounds and 1.9 blocks…Played in both the Jordan Brand Classic and Nike Hoops Summit.
